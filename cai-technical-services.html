Technical Services Overview

  • Service appointment booking and rescheduling
  • Warranty & ticket lookup with guided troubleshooting
  • Field technician dispatch coordination
  • Post-service feedback and CSAT capture

Targets: reduce call center load, increase first-contact resolution, improve CSAT.

AI Agent Capabilities

  • Natural language appointment booking with constraints
  • Knowledge-grounded troubleshooting (KB, manuals, CRM)
  • Tool-use: CRM, scheduling, notification APIs
  • Multi-lingual: TR/EN dialogs and assets

Outcomes: lower AHT, fewer transfers, higher NPS/CSAT.
