Sample Use Cases

  • Conversation Designer — filters calls by a high "Empathy Gap" score and realizes the bot's TTS needs a calmer, more empathetic tone during complaint flows.
  • QA Team — tracks high "Cognitive Load" scores (extended initial silences) to identify and rewrite confusing IVR prompts before they cause call drops.
  • Product Team — monitors "Barge-in Friction" to identify which agent blocks are too long and causing customer impatience.
Assumptions / notes:
· MaxOverlapDuration = 5.0 sec (assumed industry-typical upper bound; real value should come from production config) — used to normalize FrictionIndex.
· Sentiment scores are assumed to be in the -100..+100 range.
· BotSpeakSeverity = (Hesitation/min ÷ Speed) × (Tension/100) produces very small values due to unit mismatch; scaled ×1000 for readability (noted on the card).
· Threshold bands (green/amber/red) are chosen arbitrarily based on the observed simulation distribution, not calibrated production thresholds.
