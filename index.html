About me

Hakkımda

Building technology is the easy part; building the right thing that actually scales is hard. I focus on integrating AI, ML, and data into real-world workflows without losing sight of the end-user. My main goal as a product leader is to align cross-functional teams with business realities—so we solve genuine market problems instead of just shipping features.

Global Scale: Contributed to platforms reaching 6+ million users and expanding operations into 230 countries, directly supporting a 150% growth in revenue.

Rapid Go-to-Market: Led the 0-to-1 launch of a global ship-and-shop ecosystem. Managed four strategic domains and took the product from concept to live deployment in just 3 months.

AI & Product Strategy: Currently leading the Product Analysis team for Conversational Intelligence at SESTEK. In past roles, I've driven Agile transformations and rolled out ML, NLP, and Generative AI initiatives that fundamentally changed how our products operated.

I have a BA in Psychology and an MA in Organizational Behavior. This background means I look at product management through a human lens—both in understanding the users we build for and the team dynamics I manage.

While I hold a PMP® and a long list of Scrum credentials (PSPO™, PSM™, PAL™, etc.), my core identity is a Product Leader. I view project management frameworks simply as execution tools to bring discipline to the chaos, not as rigid rules. I believe in giving teams autonomy, relying on data over opinions, and creating spaces where good products can scale naturally.
