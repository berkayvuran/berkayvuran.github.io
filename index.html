About me

Hakkımda

In a world where AI is redefining our relationship with technology, my passion lies in shaping the next generation of intelligent products. I delve into the evolving realms of conversational and generative AI—not simply to follow trends, but to set them. Each project I take on transforms cutting-edge innovation into meaningful, real-world outcomes that drive measurable business impact.

Results-driven product leader with 7+ years of experience across AI, PropTech, telco, logistics, and enterprise software sectors. I specialize in product strategy, user experience design, and data-driven decision-making. My track record includes expanding operations to 230 countries across 3 continents, managing platforms serving 6+ million users, and delivering 150% revenue growth while significantly improving customer satisfaction metrics.

At SESTEK, I lead the strategic direction of the Conversational Analytics Product Analysis team, defining analytical standards and aligning with product vision. Previously at Emlakjet, I catalyzed the organization's Agile transformation while spearheading ML, NLP, and generative AI implementations in the PropTech domain. At Navlungo, I managed four critical business domains and led the end-to-end development of deliver.ist—a global ship & shop service—from concept to launch in just 3 months.

I hold PMP and Professional Scrum certifications (PSPO, PSM, PSU, PSD, PSFS, PAL), complemented by an MA in Organizational Behavior and a BA in Psychology from Hacettepe University. This unique combination allows me to bridge technical expertise with deep understanding of user needs and team dynamics—essential for building products that truly resonate with people.
